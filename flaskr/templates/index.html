Welcome to our application for the gym in Bolumbia University!

Many users are concerned about the covid. Therefore, to restrict the number of people in the gym, members of our gym must schedule an appointment with a specific section of the gym. Moreover, our gym welcomes sharing and learning, every user can be a coach and every coach can hold an event in our classroom.
