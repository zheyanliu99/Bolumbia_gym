Welcome to our application for the gym in Bolumbia University!

Many users are concerned about the covid. Therefore, to restrict the number of people in the gym, members of our gym must schedule an appointment with a specific section of the gym. In addition, our application places emphasis on the communication between our coach and user, users can share feed/post and follow other users. Moreover, our gym welcomes sharing and learning, every user can be a coach and every coach can hold an event in our classroom.

Main Functions

Coach and admin are also users, they have all the functions that users have and they own some special functions.

Users

  • Sign up and set up user profile
  • Check out the profile of other users
  • Schedule appointment to attend routine (i.e swimming) or special event (i.e Yoga class)
  • Share feeds and read feeds of others, can comment/like/dislike othersâ€™ feed
  • Follow other users, feeds of followed users will appear first
  • Raise questions to the question board and an admin will answer

Coach

  • All normal users can sign up to become a coach
  • Create and instruct special event (need available classroom and approval from admin)

Admin

  • Create/assign other admin account
  • Manage the status of each routine (open/closed, sanitized/unsanitized)
  • Approve/shut down special events created by coach
  • Answer questions in the question board

Place

  • Cardio room
    • the Cardio Room has nine operational treadmills, six elliptical machines, six AMT's, tow open spin cycles and a one individual Concept2 rower.
  • Strength training room
    • The strength training room includes the following equipment, Glute extension, Pull down/seated row, Abdominal back extension, Rotary torso and Rear delt/pec fly
  • Swimming pool
    • Swimming pool contains five 50 meters lane. Bathrooms, showers and lockers are available.
  • Yoga room
    • Classroom for yoga class, can also be used to hold other events.
  • Karate room
    • Classroom for karate class, can also be used to hold other events.
